# SOS Portal - COVID-19 Teleconsultation Platform

**Period:** Mar 2020 – Oct 2020
**Role:** Team Lead (17 people)

## Overview
Built a teleconsultation platform for COVID-19 patient management, launched in Brazil. One of the most intense projects — delivered a complete healthcare platform in 6 months during the pandemic peak.

## Team
Led a team of 17 people:
- 3 DevOps engineers
- 2 React Native developers
- 2 Web developers
- 4 Backend engineers
- 2 Designers
- 1 Security specialist
- 3 QA engineers

## Stack
- **Backend:** ASP.NET, REST APIs
- **Mobile:** React Native
- **Video:** Binah (video consultation platform)

## Key Achievements
- Launched for Brazilian healthcare system (still operational)
- Enabled remote medical consultations during pandemic
- Delivered in 6 months under extreme pressure
- Strategic planning, team morale management, execution coordination

## Impact
Deployed for the Brazilian healthcare system, enabling thousands of remote consultations during the critical COVID-19 period. Platform is still operational today.

## Leadership
- Managed cross-functional team across DevOps, mobile, web, backend, design, security, and QA
- Coordinated with stakeholders in high-pressure environment
- Delivered mission-critical healthcare software on tight timeline
