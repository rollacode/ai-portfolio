**Role:** AR experience developer, backend architect

## Overview
Rewards app where users earn "kicks" by scanning barcodes on shelves using Augmented Reality. Invite-only pilot integrated with Shopkick. Built from scratch with a team of 3 developers.

## Stack
- **Mobile:** Swift, SwiftUI, ARKit, MLKit
- **3D:** Unity3D (3D visualization)
- **Backend:** FastAPI, ASP.NET

## Key Achievements
- Built AR experience for barcode scanning on shelves
- Developed 3D product visualization overlays
- Integrated MLKit for barcode recognition
- Architected FastAPI backend to handle immense data volume from AR space
- Gamified retail experience that drives user engagement

## Technical Challenges
- Handling large volumes of spatial data from ARKit on iOS devices
- Real-time barcode detection and product matching
- Synchronizing 3D visualizations with physical shelf space

## Domain Expertise
- Augmented Reality (ARKit)
- Barcode recognition (MLKit)
- 3D visualization (Unity3D)
- Gamified retail experiences
